    Ariana Dumbledore was the youngest sister to Albus Dumbledore and Aberforth Dumbledore. When she was 6-years-old, muggle boys attacked Ariana because they wanted to see her perform magic. Traumatized, from then on she could not control her magical abilities. Her father seeked revenge on the muggle boys and was sent to Azkaban, while the rest of the family moved to Godric's Hollow and hid Ariana away. They did this so she would not be sent to St. Mungo's, as her instability threatened the Statute of Secrecy

    When she was 14-years-old, she caused a magical explosion whilst arguing with her mother and killed her. Eventually, she was accidentally killed by getting in the way of a duel between Aberforth, Albus, and Albus' friend Gellert Grindelwald.
