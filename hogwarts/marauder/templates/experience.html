THE MUGGLE EXPERIENCE™


For the low price of 19 Galleons per hour, you can get away from your boring wizard life and go on the vacation of a lifetime. Come hike across the most amazing non-magical wonders the muggle world has to offer!

Whether you'd prefer a relaxing walk (and yes, we are going to actually WALK) or a dangerous adventure, there is a Muggle Experience™ hike for you.

For An Adventurous Outing: The {{other.death_valley_buttes.name}}

If you enjoy trips to the Forbidden Forest, the {{other.death_valley_buttes.name}} are the destination for you. They are located in {{other.death_valley.name}}, a desert in {{other.death_valley.state}}. There are {{other.death_valley.visitors}} visitors to {{other.death_valley.name}} per year, and most of them are dementors. Don't be surprised if during your strenuous hike you happen to catch a happy dementor family enjoying a barbecue or a picnic. Be sure to bring lots of water! You don't want to get dehydrated.


For A More Moderate Climb: Rock Creek

If Hogsmeade is more your style than the Forbidden Forest, Rock Creek will be a great spot for you and your friends. Rock Creek is a hike of moderate dificulty located in Denali National Park in Alaska. After hiking the trail, cool off with a swim in the creek. If you look closely, hidden among the usual muggle-world fauna are a few scattered centaurs and woefully displaced house elves.


For A Relaxing Stroll: {{other.pinery.name}}

If your ideal Saturday afternoon is a stroll down Diagon Alley, come to {{other.pinery.name}} Hike in the {{other.guadalupe_mountains.name}}!! Located in Texas, {{other.pinery.name}} Hike is relaxing and non-strenuous. You will walk so little that by the time you reach the end of the trail you will feel like you just apparated there! And you won't come across anything more dangerous than this rather large Flobberworm.
