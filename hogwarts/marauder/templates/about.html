    ABOUT

    • The Six Potters:

    • First Leader: Amanda Steinwedel
      Second Leader: Kaelin Hooper

    Marauder's Map is dedicated to improving Muggle and Wizard relations, gathering and displaying information about the vast universe contained within the Harry Potter series. Information gathered from many sources about characters, creatures, stories, artifacts, spells, potions, locations, houses and books is organized and displayed here.
